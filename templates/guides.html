Recycling Guides

Paper and Cardboard

Recycle clean paper and cardboard. Remove any plastic wrapping or tape.

  • Newspapers and magazines
  • Cardboard boxes (flattened)
  • Office paper
  • Paper bags

Plastic

Check the recycling symbol on plastic items. Rinse containers before recycling.

  • Plastic bottles (remove caps)
  • Food containers
  • Plastic bags (check local guidelines)

Glass

Recycle glass bottles and jars. Remove lids and rinse before recycling.

  • Glass bottles
  • Glass jars
  • Note: Do not recycle window glass or ceramics

Metal

Recycle clean metal cans and aluminum foil.

  • Aluminum cans
  • Steel cans
  • Clean aluminum foil
